I am a Guardian

I think the Keirsey personality test is valid in general.
I believe so because of the types of questions that are given
The questions and responses given are straightforward which forces
the test-taker to choose 1 option that fits them best.

Looking at my own results, I think the test is 100% valid
as the characteristics of a guardian are relatable to my every-day life and morals.
Just as the test describes, I am humble, hard-working, loyal and helpful.
